We are Rooting for YOU!

Reimagining financial service impacts to women, the underserved and the communities we serve through Savings, micro-Health insurances and Education; the ‘S.H.E’ impact.

Savings
Only 10% of Nigerians have access to a structured savings plan

Our saving products are customer focused, with a goal of generating high returns for them whilst achieving their desired financial goals. Hence, a continuous product innovation strategy that is impact based is entrenched in the core of our savings strategies.

Health
About 8.5 % of children born in Nigeria do not survive beyond age 5

You guessed right, we are also rooting for your well-being! Our products are designed to give you access to low premium, basic healthcare services whilst performing your other banking activities.
