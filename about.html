Who We Are

Pecantrust Microfinance Bank Limited was incorporated in 2016 and licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria. The Bank is focused on supporting entrepreneurial development and empowerment of the economically disadvantaged micro, small and medium scale enterprises (MSME).

It provides a wide range of financial products and services to a substantial and diversified client base that includes low-end entrepreneurs, the underbanked, cooperatives, high-net-worth individuals, among others.
